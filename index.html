Our Story

A passion for origin, a craft in every cup

At Onyx Cafe, we believe exceptional coffee begins long before it reaches our grinder. It starts with deep respect for the farmers and the unique soil that yields extraordinary beans.

We exclusively source specialty-grade beans, roasted locally in small batches to highlight the complex flavor profile of each origin. For us, every extraction is an art form, and every visit is a complete sensory journey.

The Onyx Standards

  • Artisan Roasting Small-batch techniques to unlock every bean's potential.
  • Ethical Sourcing Direct trade relationships ensuring transparency and fair pay.
  • Precision Brewing Science-backed methods for a consistently perfect profile.
  • Atmospheric Sanctuary A meticulously designed space for coffee enthusiasts.
